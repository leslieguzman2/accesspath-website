Cookie Policy

How cookies and similar technologies are used on our website

Effective Date: February 6, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Access Path Innovations ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on www.accesspathinnovations.com (the "Site"). It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website operators.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which function in a comparable way.

2. Our Use of Cookies

Our Site is built as a static HTML website and does not set any first-party cookies. We do not use cookies for analytics, advertising, or user tracking.

However, we use the following cookie for managing your preferences:

Cookie Name Purpose Type Duration
cookie_consent Stores your cookie consent preference so the cookie banner is not shown repeatedly Functional (localStorage) 365 days

3. Third-Party Cookies and Services

While we do not set tracking cookies ourselves, we use third-party services that may set their own cookies or collect data when you visit our Site:

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display the Inter and Playfair Display typefaces. When you visit our Site, your browser makes requests to Google's servers to load these fonts. Google may log this data according to their privacy policy.

  • Data collected: IP address, browser user agent, referring URL
  • Purpose: Font delivery
  • More info: Google Privacy Policy

Unsplash

Blog section images are loaded from Unsplash's content delivery network (CDN). Unsplash may log standard access data when images are requested.

  • Data collected: IP address, browser user agent
  • Purpose: Image delivery
  • More info: Unsplash Privacy Policy

FormSubmit.co

Our email subscription form is processed through FormSubmit.co. When you submit the form, your email address is transmitted to their servers.

  • Data collected: Email address, form submission data
  • Purpose: Email form processing
  • More info: FormSubmit.co

GoDaddy Hosting

Our Site is hosted on GoDaddy, which may collect standard server logs including IP addresses.

  • Data collected: IP address, access logs
  • Purpose: Website hosting
  • More info: GoDaddy Privacy Policy

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. You can typically:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete individual cookies or all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies from specific sites
  • Block all cookies entirely

Please note that blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of some websites. Since our Site does not rely on first-party cookies for functionality, blocking cookies should not significantly impact your experience on our Site.

Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

Opting Out of Google Services

You can manage how Google uses your data by visiting Google's Data & Privacy settings.

5. Do Not Track

Our Site does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" browser signals because we do not engage in tracking. Since we do not set tracking cookies or use analytics services, there is no tracking behavior to modify.
